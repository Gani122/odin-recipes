White Rice

Description

Not everyone knows how to cook rice, and that's fine. But everyone CAN learn how to make it quick and easily. I'll be showing you a simple white rice recipe that's easy to remember.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • A pinch of salt
  • Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/4th of an onion
  • Bouillon cube

Steps

  1. Wash your rice! Run the rice under cool-cold water until the water runs clear
  2. Dice up the piece of onion
  3. Heat up a pot on the stove to high heat
  4. Once the pot is hot, add the olive oil
  5. Add the diced onions to the pot and stir it in to the olive oil until golden brown
  6. Add in the bouillon cube to the pot and stir until completely melted
  7. Add in the rice and "toast" it for about 30 seconds to a minute
  8. Add in the 2 cups of water and stir
  9. Add in a pinch of salt and stir
  10. Let the water come to a boil, then cover the pot and turn the stove down to low heat
  11. White rice should simmer for about 18 minutes
  12. Once done, remove it from the heat but keep it covered for another 5-10 minutes
  13. That's it! You should now have perfectly cooked rice
