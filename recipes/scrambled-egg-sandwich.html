Scrambled Egg Sandwich

Description

This is a scrambled egg sandwich that anyone can make at home! The ingredients are nice and simple and it's very easy to make. Making this sandwich should only take you about 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt
  • Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 slice of American cheese

Steps

  1. Crack and beat 3 eggs in a bowl
  2. Add a pinch of salt and stir it in
  3. Heat up a pan on the stove to medium heat
  4. Add the olive oil to the pan and move the pan around to make sure it's coated in the oil
  5. Add the eggs to the pan and constantly move them to scramble
  6. Toast your slices of bread in a toaster
  7. Add the eggs on top of a slice of toast
  8. Add a slice of cheese on top of the eggs
  9. Put the other slice of toast on top to finish the sandwich
  10. Enjoy!
