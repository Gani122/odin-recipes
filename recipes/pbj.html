PB&J Sandwich

Description

A simple peanut butter and jelly sandwich, this recipe is an all-time classic. Easy to make and always delicious, this is a perfect recipe for a quick and healthy snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon strawberry jam

Steps

  1. Toast 2 slices of bread in a toaster
  2. Spread one slice of bread with peanut butter
  3. Spread strawberry (or your choice of jelly) on the other slice of toast
  4. Combine the two pieces of toast to make a sandwich
  5. Enjoy!
